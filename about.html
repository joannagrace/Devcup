Bud-Get

An automated budget managing system.

Gives you control over your money

A budget is a way of being intentional about the way you spend and save your money. It is said that with budgeting, you control your money and not your money controls you. Budgeting saves you the stress of suddenly having to adjust to lack of funds because you did not initially plan how to spend them. It also helps you decide if you want to sacrifice short term spending.

Keeps you focused on your money goals

You avoid spending unnecessarily on items and services that do not contribute to attaining your financial goals. If you are working with limited resources, budgeting makes it easier to make ends meet.

Enables you to save for expected and unexpected costs

Budgeting allows you to plan to set aside money for emergency costs.
